Access:

Northwind Shelter can be accessed from Soul Gorge if you provide General Kohan with a Passage Ticket. Once inside, players can move on to the next map, Northwind Descent, by walking to the very bottom of Northwind Shelter. To exit the map, players can return to the starting point and click on the portal to leave​1​.
Monsters and NPCs:

Northwind Shelter is inhabited by several monsters and NPCs. The monsters include the Northwind Sh. General, Northwind Sh. Soldier, Northwind Sh. Warlock, Northwind Sh. Warrior, Shelter Skirmisher, and a mysterious character denoted as "???". The NPCs found in this area are Bounty Hunter (Shelter) and Lost Scout​1​.
Metins and Ore Veins:

The map does not have any Metins or ore veins available​1​.
Adjacent Areas:

Northwind Shelter is connected to other areas via teleporters. Northwind Descent can be accessed by walking to the very bottom of the map, and Soul Gorge can be accessed through a teleporter located at coordinates (19, 640) on the map​1​.
Level Progression:

A guide on the Seishin board suggests that Northwind Shelter is part of the progression pathway to level up to Champion Level 30, following Northwind Canyon and Soul Gorge​2​.
Northwinds Dungeon Series:

Northwind Shelter is the first of three maps in the Northwinds dungeons series, followed by Northwind Descent and ultimately leading to a secret round chamber in Northwind Deeps where players can attempt to defeat Alastor by talking to Ghostly Guardian, provided they meet certain requirements​3​.
